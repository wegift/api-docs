title: "Custom payout domain"
description: "Use your own domain for Runa payout links to provide a branded experience for your recipients."

---

<Info>
  Custom payout domains are not available by default. To enable this feature,
  contact your Account Manager.
</Info>

By default, Runa payout links are served from `spend.runa.io` and `payout.runa.io`. With a custom payout domain, your recipients will see links on your own domain instead, improving trust and brand consistency.

A custom domain will apply to all orders in your account, and all new payout links will use the custom domain automatically. Existing links will continue to function on the original Runa domains.

## Setting up your custom domain

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose a subdomain">
    Select a subdomain of a domain you own. For example, if your domain is `company.com`, you might use `example.company.com`. This subdomain must be dedicated to Runa payout links and cannot be used for other purposes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a CNAME DNS record">
    Create a `CNAME` DNS record on your chosen subdomain pointing to `spend.runa.io`.

    For example, if your subdomain is `example.company.com`:

    | Type    | Name    | Value           |
    | ------- | ------- | --------------- |
    | `CNAME` | `example` | `spend.runa.io` |

  </Step>
  <Step title="Inform Runa">
    Let your Account Manager know the subdomain you have configured. Runa will verify the DNS record, complete the setup, and apply the custom domain to your account.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Once configured, all new payout links for your account will use your custom domain automatically.
